Priorities

Accessible Information

Staff contacts, financials, and other crucial pieces of information are often nearly impossible to find. We aim to increase the transparency of OSU by streamlining the process of finding such information.

Student Clubs

Most people are aware of only one way to help clubs: increasing funding. However, as club leaders, we understand that you face space constraints and other such issues. As the only engineer in the ASOSU Senate, I know that no one is pressuring the College of Engineering to support clubs. You deserve someone backing you up. We pledge to make that happen.

Student Media

We will also expand and grow student media. In addition to keeping student media connected with clubs, we aim to increase coverage of the inner-workings of the University to benefit all students.
